About Me

Personal

I spent the first nineteen years of life in Rockford Illinois where I grew up with my seven siblings. I enlisted in the Air Force shortly after graduating high school. Through the Air Force I gained incredible experiences from jumping out of planes thirteen thousand feet in the air to scuba diving one-hundred and fifty feet below the surface. I also met my wife the year before I separated the military on a passenger plane(she was my flight attendant). After I separated, my wife and I moved to the greater Seattle area which is where I now reside.

Professional

Through the military I have grown a wide professional background. I am a certified air traffic controller which has taught me to handle stress and task saturation. I have handled various types of communications equipment with high levels of encryption. I have organized training events for hundreds of airmen. Most importantly, I have learned the value and significance of teamwork.

Education

I have completed classes from Ashford University and North Seattle College. Through those classes I have accrued a baseline understanding of Python, JavaScript, MySQL and Java. I have complete Code 102 from Code Fellows and am currently self learning c-sharp.
